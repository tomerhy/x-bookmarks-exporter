Privacy Policy

App: X Video URL Exporter (THY Production)

Last updated: 2026-01-21

Overview

This extension extracts video URLs from X (Twitter) pages that you view and stores them locally in your browser so you can review and export them.

Data We Collect

  • Video URLs from pages you visit on x.com or twitter.com.

How We Use Data

  • To show the gallery view and allow export/copy of captured URLs.
  • To enable batch auto-scroll for bookmarks and channel pages.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. We do not transmit, sell, or share your data with any third party.

Permissions Justification

  • Host permissions (x.com, twitter.com, video.twimg.com): required to detect video URLs and load media hosted on X.
  • Storage: required to save captured URLs locally.
  • Tabs: required to open the gallery in a new tab and interact with the active tab.
  • Scripting: required to inject the content script when you start auto-scroll.
